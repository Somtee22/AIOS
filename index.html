Frequently Asked Questions

Website security is crucial for safeguarding your online presence against cyber threats such as malware and vulnerabilities. With Soteria's comprehensive website security solutions, you benefit from daily scans, automated malware removal, vulnerability patching, and a robust web application firewall that blocks harmful traffic.

Every website, regardless of size, is vulnerable to hacking. Being proactive with security measures is far better than reacting to breaches later. On average, websites face a staggering 58 cyberattacks per day, making preemptive security essential. Explore our blog on 'Why Do I Need Website Security?' for more insights.

Website backups are a vital part of security. They offer a simple way to save a complete copy of your website, including files and databases. With just a click, you can restore your website in case of accidental or malicious code corruption.

Stay vigilant against common website security threats like brute force attacks, cross-site scripting (XSS), DDoS attacks, phishing attempts, malware infections, SQL injections, and more.

To safeguard your site against malware, ensure regular updates for your CMS, themes, and plugins. Regularly clean your hosting account, avoid leaving backups on the same server, use strong and regularly updated passwords, and prioritize proactive security measures.

While SSL certificates encrypt data between your site and visitors, they don't fully protect against all online threats. Enhance your security with Soteria's comprehensive solutions to prevent defacement, resource theft, and other cyberattacks.

It's a common misconception that hosting providers are solely responsible for website security. While they secure the server your site is hosted on, the responsibility for securing the website itself falls on the website owner. It's akin to securing an apartment building where property management secures the building, but each tenant must secure their own apartment.
